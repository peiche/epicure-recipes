Asian Omelet

  • 10 min
  • 1 serving
  • $1.40/serving

Make healthy, fluffy, restaurant-quality omelets with less fuss.

Ingredients

2 eggs
2 tbsp milk
12 cup stir-fry vegetable blend, fresh or frozen
1 tspAsian Stir-Fry Seasoning
hot sauce, to taste (optional)

Preparation

  1. Whisk eggs and milk until frothy. Pour into a lightly oiled Omelet Maker.
  2. Top eggs with vegetables and Asian Stir-Fry Seasoning.
  3. Close lid and microwave on high for 23 minutes.
  4. Loosen omelet with a spatula, flip onto a plate and serve topped with hot sauce, if desired.

Nutritional Information

Per serving: Calories 260, Fat 13 g (Saturated 4 g, Trans 0 g), Cholesterol 490 mg, Sodium 220 mg, Carbohydrate 17 g (Fiber 4 g, Sugars 3 g), Protein 19 g.
