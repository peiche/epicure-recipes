Protein-Packed Chicken & Broccoli Frittata
20 min
4 servings
$2.37/serving
Ingredients

Serve With –

Preparation
  1. In a Round or Multipurpose Steamer add chicken and microwave on high for 4-6 min.
  2. Add broccoli and 3 Onion Dip Mix and steam for an additional 2-3 min.
  3. Drizzle with olive oil, and season with pepper and Balsamic Vinaigrette Dressing Mix. Set it aside.
  4. In a large bowl combine eggs, oat flour, baking powder and 1⁄2 cup cheese.
  5. Pour mixture over chicken and broccoli and top with remaining 1⁄2 cup cheese.
  6. Cover and microwave on high for 4-5 min, until eggs are set.  
  7. Cool, slice and serve. While cooling, toast and butter bread.
Nutritional Information

Per serving: Calories 510, Fat 30 g (Saturated 8 g, Trans 0 g), Cholesterol 435 mg, Sodium 590 mg, Carbohydrate 34 g (Fibre 4 g, Sugars 5 g), Protein 34 g.
