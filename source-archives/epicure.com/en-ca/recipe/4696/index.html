Lemon Dilly Vinaigrette

  • 2 min
  • 1/2 cup
  • $0.45/serving

This classic, all-purpose lemon-dill dressing is not only great on salads, but also on potatoes or as a marinade for fish or grilled chicken.

Perfectly Balance Your Plate

Serve with 1 serving protein, 1 serving grain/starch, and 2 cups greens.

Ingredients

14 cup fresh lemon juice
14 cup oil
finely grated zest of one lemon, optional
1 tbspLemon Dilly Dip Mix
1 tsp Dijon-style mustard
14 tsp sugar
Sea Salt (Grinder), to taste
Black Pepper (Grinder), to taste

Preparation

  1. In a Cruet combine all ingredients and shake to mix.

Nutritional Information

Per serving (1 tbsp): Calories 60, Fat 7 g (Saturated 1 g, Trans 0 g), Cholesterol 0 mg, Sodium 130 mg, Carbohydrate 1 g (Fibre 0 g, Sugars 1 g), Protein 0.1 g.

Tips

Refrigerate up to 1 week.
