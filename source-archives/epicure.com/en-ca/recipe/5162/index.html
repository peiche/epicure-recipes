Apple Pie Latte

  • 13 min
  • 4 servings
  • $0.65/serving

Everything you love about apple pie served up in a warm, comforting latte.

Ingredients

1 cup water
12 cup sugar
23 tspApple Pie Spice
2 cups brewed coffee
1 tsp vanilla extract
1 cup 2% milk


Preparation

  1. In an Multipurpose Pot - 8 C bring water, sugar, and Apple Pie Spice to a boil over medium-high heat to make a syrup.
  2. Meanwhile, prepare coffee.
  3. Once syrup mixture is boiling, stir for 1 min, whisking frequently. Remove from heat and stir in vanilla.
  4. Warm milk in microwave on high, about 1 min. Using a milk frother or whisk, beat until frothy.
  5. Pour coffee into cups. Stir 1 tbsp syrup into each cup (refrigerate leftovers in a Cruet for use another day). Top with frothed milk. Dust with more apple pie spice, if desired.

Nutritional Information

Per serving: Calories 170, Fat 1 g (Saturated 0 g, Trans 0 g), Cholesterol 5 mg, Sodium 30 mg, Carbohydrate 23 g (Fiber 1 g, Sugars 21 g), Protein 2 g.

Tips

Swap 2% milk with soy or almond milk for a non-dairy alternative.
