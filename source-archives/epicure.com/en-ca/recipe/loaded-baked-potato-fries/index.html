Loaded Baked Potato Fries

  • 55 min
  • 6 servings
  • $1.55/serving

It's going to be messy and delicious.

Ingredients

Fries

4 - 6 potatoes
14 cup olive oil
Sea Salt, to taste
Black Pepper, to taste

Ranch Sauce

1 cup sour cream
14 cup milk
2 tbspCreamy Ranch Dip Mix

Toppings

1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
12 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
SPG SeasoningorCCB Dip Mix, to taste
14 cup green onions

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 400 F (200 C).
  2. Cut potatoes into thick steak-cut slices. Place on aSheet Panlined with aSheet Pan Liner.
  3. Drizzle with olive oil and lightly toss using aGrip & Grab. Sprinkle with salt and pepper, to taste.
  4. Bake for 40 minutes or until crispy.
  5. In a bowl, whisk sour cream, milk, and Creamy Ranch Dip Mix. Drizzle on potatoes.
  6. Sprinkle with toppings.
  7. Set oven to broil. Place loaded fries under the broiler for a few seconds to melt cheese.

Nutritional Information

Per serving: Calories 430, Fat 20 g (Saturated 5 g, Trans 0 g), Cholesterol 25 mg, Sodium 380 mg, Carbohydrate 48 g (Fibre 5 g, Sugars 5 g), Protein 15 g.
