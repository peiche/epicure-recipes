Country Skillet

  • 20 min
  • 4 servings
  • $1.62/serving

Have breakfast for brunch, lunch, and dinner!

Ingredients

1 tbsp olive oil
1 small potato
3 tbsp water
1 cupPoco Picante Salsa
1 cup chopped cooked ham, chicken or turkey
4 eggs
1 tbspCCB Dip Mix

Preparation

  1. Heat oil in frying pan over medium-high heat. Add potato slices and stir-fry until lightly golden. Add water and simmer for 23 minutes, until potatoes absorb liquid.
  2. Stir in salsa and ham. Stir often for about 35 minutes, until heated through.
  3. Meanwhile, stir eggs with dip mix. Pour into warm potato mixture and stir for 23 minutes, until eggs are scrambled.

Nutritional Information

Per serving: Calories 290, Fat 16 g (Saturated 4 g, Trans 0 g), Cholesterol 300 mg, Sodium 450 mg, Carbohydrate 12 g (Fibre 2 g, Sugars 3 g), Protein 25 g.
