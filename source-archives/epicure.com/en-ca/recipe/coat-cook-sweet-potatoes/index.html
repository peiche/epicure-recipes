Coat & Cook Sweet Potatoes

  • 20 - 25 min
  • 8 servings
  • $0.40/serving

Perfectly Balance Your Plate

Serve 12 cup with 1 serving protein, 1 serving grains, 2 cups veggies, and 3 tbsp Epicure dressing of your choice.

Ingredients

2 lbs (900 g) sweet potatoes, cubed
1 tbsp oil
1 pkg Southern Baked Gluten Free Crumb Mix, or crumb mix of your choice

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 425 F (220 C).
  2. Lightly coat sweet potatoes with oil.
  3. Place mix in resealable plastic bag. Add sweet potatoes, shaking gently until evenly coated.
  4. Place on aSheet Panlined with aSheet Pan Liner. Cook 1520 minutes.

Nutritional Information

Per serving: Calories 70, Fat 0.4 g (Saturated 0.1 g, Trans 0 g), Cholesterol 0 mg, Sodium 50 mg, Carbohydrate 15 g (Fibre 3 g, Sugars 5 g), Protein 1 g.

Tips

Cook once, eat twice:Leftovers are perfect for dinner and lunch made in minutes. Simply portion and add to your favourite nourish bowls, soups, and salads.
