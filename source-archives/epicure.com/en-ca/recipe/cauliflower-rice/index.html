Cauliflower Rice
8 min
4 servings
$1.45/serving
Ingredients
Preparation
  1. Using a grater or a food processor, "rice" cauliflower until it resembles rice grains.
  2. Place cauliflower in a Multipurpose Steamer and microwave on high for 46 minutes.
  3. Sprinkle with seasoning.
