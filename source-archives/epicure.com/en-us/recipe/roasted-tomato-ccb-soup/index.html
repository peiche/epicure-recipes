Roasted Tomato CCB Soup
1 hour
10 servings
$0.49/serving
Ingredients
Preparation
  1. Preheat oven to 425 F. Line aSheet Panwith aSheet Pan Liner.
  2. Place tomatoes and onion in a bowl; drizzle with 3 tbsp seasoning, oil, vinegar, salt, and pepper. Toss to mix; spread out on Sheet Pan Liner.
  3. Bake until tomatoes are soft, juicy, and starting to burst, about 30 min.
  4. Scrape mixture into aMultipurpose Pot; add broth. Bring to a boil; reduce heat and simmer for 10 min to let flavours blend. Working in batches, pure in a blender until smooth. Return soup to pot; stir in remaining 2 tbsp dip mix. Reheat over medium-high, stirring often, until warm. If making ahead, refrigerate up to 3 days or portion and freeze up to 3 months.
  5. To serve, stir yogurt with aioli mix and milk. Spoon soup into bowls and top with a swirl of yogurt mixture. Top with Better Than Bacon Topper , if desired.
Perfectly Balance Your Plate

Serve with 1 slice whole wheat or sourdough bread with 1 tbsp butter or 2 tbsp avocado. Add a boiled egg in your soup to up your protein.

Nutritional Information

Per serving (1 cup): Calories 50, Fat 3 g (Saturated 0.5 g, Trans 0 g), Cholesterol 0 mg, Sodium 160 mg, Carbohydrate 4 g (Fiber 1 g, Sugars 2 g), Protein 2 g.
