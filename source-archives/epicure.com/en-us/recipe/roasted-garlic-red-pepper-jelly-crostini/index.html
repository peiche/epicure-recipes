Roasted Garlic & Red Pepper Jelly Crostini

  • 15 min
  • 12 slices
  • $0.83/serving

Ingredients

12 slices French baguette
1 - 2 tbsp olive oil
12 cup soft goat cheese or Fresh cheese
12 cupRed Pepper Jelly
12 recipe preparedRoasted Garlic Bulbs

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 375 F (190 C).
  2. Place baguette slices on a crisper. Lightly brush with oil. Bake until toasted, 5 minutes per side.
  3. Spread goat cheese on baguette slices. Mash in a clove of Roasted Garlic Bulbs on each. Top with Red Pepper Jelly.

Nutritional Information

Per serving: Calories 160, Fat 4.5 g (Saturated 1.5 g, Trans 0 g), Cholesterol 5 mg, Sodium 220 mg, Carbohydrate 24 g (Fiber 1 g, Sugars 6 g), Protein 4 g.
