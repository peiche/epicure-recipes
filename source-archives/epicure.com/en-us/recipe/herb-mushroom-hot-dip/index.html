Herb & Mushroom Hot Dip

  • 10 min
  • 2 cups
  • $0.47/serving

Ingredients

1 pkgHerb & Mushroom Hot Dip Mix (Pack of 3)
1 cup cream cheese, at room temperature
1 cup light mayonnaise

Preparation

  1. In a bowl, combine dip mix with cream cheese and mayonnaise.
  2. Scrape into a heat-safe dish. Microwave, uncovered, on medium 3-5 min or until warm. Or bake in preheated 375 F oven 1520 min or until warm and bubbly.

Nutritional Information

Per serving (2 tbsp): Calories 110, Fat 10 g (Saturated 4 g, Trans 0 g), Cholesterol 20 mg, Sodium 170 mg, Carbohydrate 3 g (Fiber 0 g, Sugars 1 g), Protein 2 g.

Tips

Spread dip on toasted baguette slices and top with grated cheese; broil until melted.
