Roasted Red Pepper and Tomato Zuppa

  • 13 min
  • 4 servings
  • $0.59/serving

A soothing bowl of pure, flavourful comfort, ready in less than 10 minutes.

Ingredients

1 can (14 oz/398 ml) low-sodium diced tomatoes
4 cupsNourish Broth, prepared
2 tbsp Roasted Red Pepper Dip Mix
Sea Salt (Grinder), to taste
Black Pepper (Grinder), to taste
14 cupPesto Sauce, prepared and divided optional

Preparation

  1. InMultipurpose Pot, whisk tomatoes, Nourish Broth, and Roasted Red Pepper Dip Mix.
  2. Bring to a simmer for 5 minutes.
  3. Season to taste with Salt and Pepper and add a swirl of Pesto to each serving, if desired. Serve immediately.

Nutritional Information

Per serving: Calories 40, Fat 0.1 g (Saturated 0 g, Trans 0 g), Cholesterol 0 mg, Sodium 110 mg, Carbohydrate 8 g (Fiber 2 g, Sugars 4 g), Protein 2 g.

Tips

Top with grated Parmesan cheese orGarlic Croutons.

Serve with a grilled cheese sandwich.
