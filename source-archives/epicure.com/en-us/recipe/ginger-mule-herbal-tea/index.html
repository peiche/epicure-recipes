Ginger Mule Herbal Tea

  • 5 min
  • 1 serving
  • $0.68/serving

Ingredients

1 cup boiling water
1-2 bags Ginger Mule Herbal Tea Blend
12 cup club soda, optional

Preparation

  1. Pour boiling water over tea bag. Steep 57 min.
  2. Chill for iced tea. Top with club soda, if using.

Nutritional Information

Per serving: Calories 0, Fat 0 g (Saturated 0 g, Trans 0 g), Cholesterol 0 mg, Sodium 10 mg, Carbohydrate 0 g (Fiber 0 g, Sugars 0 g), Protein 0 g.

Tips

For a refreshing summer cocktail, add a shot of vodka to chilled tea.

Try cold brewed iced tea. Fill a pitcher with cold water. For every 1 cup of water, add 1 tea bag. Refrigerate at least 2 hours or overnight. Remove tea bags and enjoy for up to 4 days!
