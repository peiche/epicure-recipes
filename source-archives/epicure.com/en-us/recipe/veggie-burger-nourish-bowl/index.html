Veggie Burger Nourish Bowl

  • 15 min
  • 4 servings
  • $3.56/serving

This is the most delicious veggie burger you've ever tasted!

Perfectly Balance Your Plate

This is a perfectly balanced plate.

Ingredients

2 tomatoes
12 avocado
12 red onion
4 cups baby spinach or mixed greens
2 cups zucchini noodles
1 recipe preparedVeggie Burger, made into sliders
1 cup shredded cheddar
34 cup preparedBalsamic Vinaigrette Dressing

Preparation

  1. Cut tomatoes into wedges, slice avocado and slice onion into rings.
  2. Arrange tomatoes, avocado, onion, baby spinach, zucchini, Veggie Burger sliders, and shredded cheddar in four bowls.
  3. Drizzle with dressing and enjoy!

Nutritional Information

Per serving: Calories 430, Fat 18 g (Saturated 3 g, Trans 0 g), Cholesterol 5 mg, Sodium 770 mg, Carbohydrate 46 g (Fiber 14 g, Sugars 10 g), Protein 31 g.
