Turmeric & Ginger Elixir

  • 5 min
  • 2 servings
  • $2.50/serving

Ingredients

1 cup Turmeric & Ginger Blend Enhanced Herbal Tea, steeped
1 cup kombucha (ginger or berry flavored)
14 cup bourbon or rum, optional
crushed ice
2 slices lemon or orange
candied ginger, optional, optional
fresh mint, optional

Preparation

  1. Stir together tea, kombucha, and bourbon, if desired.
  2. Fill glasses with ice; pour in tea mixture.
  3. Garnish with fresh lemon, skewers of candied ginger, and mint, if desired.

Nutritional Information

Per serving: Calories 25, Fat 0 g (Saturated 0 g, Trans 0 g), Cholesterol 0 mg, Sodium 0 mg, Carbohydrate 5 g (Fiber 0 g, Sugars 5 g), Protein 0 g.

Tips

Rim glasses with ground Turmeric & Black Pepper Blend.
