Power Up Bliss Balls

  • 12 min
  • 12 balls
  • $2.35/serving

Make ahead, freeze, then grab and go for an easy breakfast.

Perfectly Balance Your Plate

This is a perfectly balanced plate.

Ingredients

1 cup packed medjool dates, about 12 dates
12 cup nut butter such as natural peanut butter, almond, or nut-free alternative
12 cup hemp hearts
2 scoopsPower Up Chocolate Protein BlendorPower Up Vanilla Protein Blend
4 tbsp milk, or dairy-free alternative

Toppings (optional): Summer Berry Sweet Dip Mix or shredded coconut.

Preparation

  1. Remove pits from dates; coarsely chop. Place dates, nut butter, hemp hearts, protein powder, and milk in a food processor. Pulse just until combined and dough is slightly sticky, about 2030 sec.
  2. Portion dough into 12 balls; roll in desired toppings.
  3. Refrigerate up to 5 days or freeze up to 2 months.

Nutritional Information

Per serving (2 balls): Calories 390, Fat 17 g (Saturated 2.5 g, Trans 0 g), Cholesterol 0 mg, Sodium 135 mg, Carbohydrate 44 g (Fiber 6 g, Sugars 34 g), Protein 17 g.

Tips

Are you a chocoholic? For an extra fudgy kick, add 1 tbsp cocoa powder when blending.

For easy portioning, use a small ice cream scoop to form balls.

Use soy or almond milk for a dairy-free alternative.
