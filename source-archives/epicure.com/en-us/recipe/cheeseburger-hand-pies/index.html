Cheeseburger Hand Pies
30 min
8 servings
$1.91/serving

Thank you, Jenn Nguyen, for this recipe! These hand pies bring together the best of two worlds: the savoury goodness of a cheeseburger inside a hot and flaky pastry. Set up a DIY burger bar with these hand pies taking centre stage—serve with crispy fries, sliced veggies, and your choice of condiments for dunking.

Ingredients
Preparation
  1. Preheat oven to 400° F. Line Sheet Pan with Sheet Pan Liner.  
  2. Chop pickles into small pieces. 
  3. In Sauté Pan, brown beef over medium-high heat. Add pickles, seasoning, cheese, water, and ketchup; stir to combine. Remove from heat and set aside.  
  4. Cut each puff pastry sheet into 8 equal pieces (you should have 16 pieces in total).  
  5. Arrange half of the pieces on the pan. Top each of the 8 pieces with an equal portion of beef filling, then place one of the remaining 8 pieces over each filling-topped piece. Pinch edges to close to form hand pies. Use a fork to poke holes on top of each hand pie. Brush with melted butter or oil.  
  6. Bake 12–14 min, or until golden brown.  
Nutritional Information

Per serving (1 hand pie): Calories 510, Fat 34 g (Saturated 11 g, Trans 0.4 g), Cholesterol 45 mg, Sodium 470 mg, Carbohydrate 32 g (Fiber 2 g, Sugars 2 g), Protein 19 g.
