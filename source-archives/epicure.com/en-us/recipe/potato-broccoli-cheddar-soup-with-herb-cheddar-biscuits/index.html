Potato & Broccoli Cheddar Soup with Herb & Cheddar Biscuits

  • 30 min
  • 6 servings
  • $3.92/serving

Thank you, Stephanie Kell for this recipe!

Ingredients

SOUP

2 cups diced potatoes
1 12 cups diced carrots
2 cups broccoli
2 cups boiling water
1 tbsp Nourish Broth Mix
1 tbsp Vegetable Broth Mix
1 pkg Home-Style Chicken Stew Seasoning
2 cups milk, your choice
1 tbsp3 Onion Dip Mix
3 tbspCCB Dip Mix
1 tspMinced Garlic
2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
Black Pepper (Grinder), to taste

BISCUITS

oil
1 12 cupsEasy Peasy Bagel, Pancake & Waffle Mix
14 tsp salt
1 cup 2% plain Greek yogurt
12 tbspHerb & Garlic Dip Mix
12 cup shredded cheddar cheese
1 egg yolk
1 tsp water

Preparation

SOUP

  1. Place your chopped potatoes, carrots, and broccoli in the Multipurpose Steamer with 2 cups of boiling water, Nourish BrothMix, and Veggie BrothMix. Cover with lid and steam in microwave for 10 mins.
  2. Mixstew seasoninginto 2 cups of milk, set aside.
  3. Add in3 Onion and CCB Dip Mixesand Minced Garlic. Cover and cook for additional 2 mins.
  4. Using a potato masher, mash the potatoes, carrots, and broccoli until they are in very small pieces(shouldresemble lumpy mashed potatoes).
  5. Pour in seasoned milk, mix well. Microwave for an additional 2 min intervals, stirring every 2 mins. Continue to microwave until it becomes thick.
  6. Stir in shredded cheese;add pepper to taste.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy!

BISCUITS

  1. Preheat oven to 375.
  2. Combine bagel mix and salt in a bowl.
  3. Mix inGreekyogurt and dip mix, until a doughforms.
  4. Work in shredded cheese.
  5. Form into biscuit shape and place on a Sheet Pan lined with aSheet Pan Linerthat has been brushed with oil.
  6. Mix egg yolk with 1 tsp water,andbrushontopof biscuits.
  7. Bake for 1820 mins

Nutritional Information

Per serving: Calories 520, Fat 21 g (Saturated 11 g, Trans 0.5 g), Cholesterol 90 mg, Sodium 1110 mg, Carbohydrate 61 g (Fiber 7 g, Sugars 9 g), Protein 25 g.

Tips

This can also be made on the stovetop! Follow the same instructions;however,instead of microwaving,simply allow the mixture to simmer! I highly recommend steaming your veggies in your Multipurpose Steamer first to speed up the process!
