Best Ever Veggie Burger

  • 15 min
  • 4 servings
  • $0.98/serving

Ingredients

1 can (19 oz/540 g) chickpeas, drained and rinsed
1 small zucchini, grated
2 slices sandwich bread
14 cup crunchy peanut or almond butter
2 tbspBig Burger Sauce, prepared
1 tbspBurger Seasoning
1 tbsp vegetable oil, for grilling
4 buns, split
1 recipePoco Picante Salsa, prepared, optional

Preparation

  1. In a food processor, or with a masher, combine first six ingredients. Pulse until roughly chopped.
  2. Form the mixture into four patties. Patties can be stored in the refrigerator for up to two days.
  3. Heat oil in a frying pan on medium, and pan-fry patties for approximately 5 minutes per side, until crisp and brown on the outside and warmed through.
  4. Place on bottom halves of buns, then divide sauce over top. Add toppings and top of buns and serve.

Nutritional Information

Per serving: Calories 450, Fat 14 g (Saturated 2 g, Trans 0 g), Cholesterol 0 mg, Sodium 820 mg, Carbohydrate 67 g (Fiber 13 g, Sugars 6 g), Protein 18 g.

Tips

Draining and rinsing your chickpeas can cut your sodium content by almost half.
