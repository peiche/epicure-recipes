White Bean and Roasted Garlic Dip

  • 10 min
  • 2 cups
  • $0.16/serving

This smooth and creamy dip is loaded with flavour â€“ perfect for dipping or topping crostinis.

Ingredients

2 tspRoasted Garlic Aioli MixorRoasted Garlic Bulbs
1â„4 cup warm water
3 tbsp olive oil, plus extra for garnish, if desired
1 can (14 oz /398 ml) white beans, drained and rinsed
1 tbsp lemon juice
1 tspMarinara Sauce Mix
Sea Salt, to taste
Black Pepper, to taste

Preparation

  1. Combine Roasted Garlic Aioli Mix, water and oil in a measuring cup. Let stand for 5 minutes.
  2. In a food processor, combine next 3 ingredients, including oil mixture, and blend until smooth. Season to taste with Sea Salt and Black Pepper.
  3. Spoon into a serving dish and drizzle with additional olive oil, if desired.

Nutritional Information

Per serving (2 tbsp): Calories 50, Fat 2.5 g (Saturated 0.4 g, Trans 0 g), Cholesterol 0 mg, Sodium 2 mg, Carbohydrate 5 g (Fiber 1 g, Sugars 0 g), Protein 2 g.

Tips

This is a great dish to take to a potluck: spoon into a short, wide-mouth canning jar, drizzle with olive oil and garnish with a couple of olives. Simply unscrew the cap and serve.

Serve with crackers, vegetables or bread sticks.
